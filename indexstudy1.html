Case Study #1 Creation of a Recycling App

The Problem: What we discovered as a team was that most people wanted to recycle and wanted to do there part in keeping the planet clean. We also discovered that people will only recycle if it is easy to do. That was the goal of the team to make recycling easy.

The Solution: We started by asking questions, Why do you recycle, why don't you recycle, what do you recycle, where is the closest recycling center. From here we were able to create a User Persona and build an app that made recycling easy.

What I Learned: 1. Ask the questions: I did not realize that most communities make it preaty diffacult to recycle. By asking questions we were able to determine the app priorities 1 Locations of recycling centers and directions to the recylcing centers 2. What can I recycle and 3. Education about Recycling information about why recycling is important.

What I Learned: 2. Make it look good: The look and feel of the app is important. You do not want to make a recycling app look like a banking app.

What I Learned: 3. Warm Up: The team did a lot of inVision Mood board work before we started designing, we felt it was important to create some thing that is inviting colorful and easy to use.

What I Learned: 4. Test that sucker: We tested the app both in and out of class I found this hard to do, simply becacause you invest so much in to it. By testing we were able to build a more solid product.

Summary Working with this team was humbling, listining to other peoples ideas and folowing someone elses lead was hard but also rewarding to see the final product with all the blame or glory spread eavenly through out the team.
